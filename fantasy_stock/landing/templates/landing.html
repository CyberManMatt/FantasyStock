Daily Fantasy Against AI

Like daily fantasy but don't want to play with others? Play daily fantasy games for the season against our AI for EXP using the players in your portfolio.

Competitive Daily Fantasy

Or play daily fantasy games against others and climb your way to the top of the ladder.

Gambling Free

Fantasy Stock uses virtual currency. You don't win real money, instead you earn virtual coins by winning daily fantasy games or just selling your player stock instances at a higher price for a profit. Buy low sell high!

Players Stock Market

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the basic types of questions for our customers. For more
information please contact us.

Q.

Which leagues will this cover?

Currently, we only have access to sports data from the NBA. Other leagues such as NFL, MLB, and NHL will be supported in the future.

Q.

Do I have to pay?

Fantasy Stock is dedicated to bring a completely free experience. Sign up for free, and start playing.

Q.

I want to contribute to the development of Fantasy Stock. How can I?

Please visit the Fantasy Stock GitHub repo to find ways to contribute to this project.

Q.

Will there be a mobile app? Or desktop app?

We plan on developing a desktop and mobile app in the near future.
